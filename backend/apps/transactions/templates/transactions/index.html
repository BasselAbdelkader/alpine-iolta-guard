The Transactions page records all money movements in and out of your trust account.

Recording Transactions

Click "Add New Transaction" to record:

  • Deposits: Client funds received into trust account
  • Withdrawals: Payments made to vendors or clients
  • Transfers: Money moved between accounts
Transaction Status

Track whether transactions are:

  • Cleared: Processed by the bank
  • Uncleared: Pending bank processing
Transaction Items

Each transaction can have multiple items linking to specific clients, cases, or vendors for detailed tracking.

Actions
  • View: See transaction details and items
  • Edit: Update transaction information
  • Clear: Mark transaction as cleared
